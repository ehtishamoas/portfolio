Aquarium Comic

Go to the Project Website
click on the axolotl to see the project description

In this project, we have created an interactive comic within a website. This short comic is centered around an aquarium, where an axolotl named Moby has arrived. He was excited to meet the inhabitants of this new aquarium, but they were all sad about their life in an aquarium, wishing for an escape. It is shown to the readers that the aquarium glass is fragile and can break even on knocking. The readers can then choose to break the glass by simulating knocking effect using mouse. This way, the reader would help the inhabitants to get freedom from the aquarium as per they wished. But is this really what they wished for?

Faces Behind D2

Go to the Project Website
click on the pizza to see the project description

In this project, we made a film that tells the story of our campus east restaurant D2, which is well-known to everyone, yet most of the things about it fall behind the scenes. Our film includes some scenes showing certain places in D2 and some interviews of staff members and students sharing their thoughts and favorite memories of D2. We also created a d2-themed website to house our film and highlight the cool food offerings and issue of food wastage.
